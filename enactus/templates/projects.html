Women Empowerment & Sustainable Fashion

Project Navodaya

Project Navodaya is dedicated to empowering unemployed women by teaching them crocheting and other valuable vocational skills to help them earn an independent livelihood. The initiative strongly emphasizes sustainability and circular economy principles by utilizing old clothes and textile waste to create upcycled fashion products for sale. By fostering skill development and providing a direct avenue for income generation, the project helps women build confidence and secure financial independence.

Education & Skill Development

Project Astitva

Project Astitva focuses on the holistic skill development of youth and individuals by providing a robust platform for learning and self-expression. Through dedicated sessions and workshops, participants develop highly practical skills, including blog writing, article creation, and other professional competencies. A cornerstone of this initiative is the publication of an annual magazine that explores and raises awareness about pressing social topics, giving a powerful voice to the community's thoughts, ideas, and stories.

250+ Students Trained 6 Editions Published

Environment & Sustainability

Project Vriksh

Project Vriksh focuses on environmental sustainability through tree plantation drives, waste management initiatives, and awareness campaigns. The project encourages communities to adopt responsible environmental practices and participate in conservation efforts. By promoting ecological responsibility and sustainable habits, the initiative aims to contribute to a greener and healthier future.
