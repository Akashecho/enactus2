Latest Release: Unspoken

ASTITVA Edition 6 (2025)

Human Rights: Global Crisis

Gutargu Astitva Edition 5 (8.0)

Gutargu Edition 5 is a harrowing look into the global crisis of Human Trafficking, described as modern-day slavery that transcends all racial and social boundaries. This edition exposes the "uncut panorama" of bondage, detailing how millions are coerced into forced labor and sexual exploitation through fraud and deception.

By highlighting the severe physical and psychological scars left on survivors, the magazine emphasizes the urgent need for awareness of human rights and legal protections like Article 23 of the Indian Constitution. It serves as a call to action, providing red flags for identification and spotlighting NGOs like Prajwala and Prerana that lead the fight for rescue and rehabilitation.

Economic Resilience

Gutargu Edition 4 (7.0)

Gutargu Edition 4 shifts the spotlight to the economic and human toll of the global health crisis, specifically examining the effects of the pandemic on small businesses. This edition documents the resilience and struggle of local enterprises navigating lockdowns, supply chain disruptions, and shifting consumer behaviors.

By highlighting the vulnerability of the unorganized sector and the innovative ways entrepreneurs adapted to survive, the magazine serves as both a record of a historic era and a call to support the backbone of our economy.

Equality & Dignity

Guturgu Edition 3 (6.0)

Gutargu Edition 3 is a comprehensive exploration of the LGBTQ+ community, focusing on the journey from historical roots to the modern-day fight for dignity. This edition tackles the complexities of identity, highlighting landmark legal victories like the decriminalization of Section 377 in India alongside the global impact of reformers such as Marsha P. Johnson and Harvey Milk.

By addressing the persistent stigmas of discrimination and the emotional hardships of "coming out," it serves as a call for radical empathy and social revolution.
