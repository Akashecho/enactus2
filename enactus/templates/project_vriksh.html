Overview

Rooting Change Through Floral Waste Upcycling

Project Vriksh (meaning "Tree" in Sanskrit) is an Enactus-led sustainability initiative focused on transforming floral waste into durable, value-based products through structured upcycling.

With nearly 800 million tons of floral waste generated globally each year, a significant portion is discarded after single use. When unmanaged, this waste contributes to odor, hygiene issues, and environmental strain. Project Vriksh addresses this challenge by converting discarded flowers into preserved, market-ready products through structured upcycling.

The initiative operates on a circular approach where floral waste is collected, processed, and transformed into products that extend the lifecycle of natural materials. Alongside upcycling efforts, the project also promotes environmental awareness through plantation drives and community engagement activities to encourage long-term ecological responsibility.

Our Impact

Project Vriksh Impact

Project Vriksh has successfully demonstrated a working waste-to-value model by collecting discarded floral waste and converting it into durable, market-ready products. The initiative validates that structured upcycling can generate both environmental and social impact while promoting responsible waste management practices.

The Challenges

The Problem We Address

  • Large volumes of floral waste generated daily
  • Improper disposal leading to odor and hygiene concerns
  • Single-use cultural and event-based flower consumption
  • Limited structured systems for floral waste reuse

These challenges highlight the need for a regenerative system that extends the lifecycle of natural materials instead of discarding them.

The Framework

Our Model

Project Vriksh follows a structured resin-based upcycling framework:

1
Collection of floral waste from local sources
2
Careful sorting and controlled drying
3
Resin encapsulation to preserve flowers permanently
4
Conversion into durable, aesthetic, and market-ready products

This process transforms short-lived organic waste into long-lasting value-based goods, ensuring both environmental impact and revenue generation.
